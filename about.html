Who We Are

LUXTRIP is a premium executive transfer service dedicated to providing luxurious, reliable and professional transportation across Nairobi and its surroundings. We specialise in delivering a seamless travel experience for executives, travellers, high-profile individuals and special events.

Founded on the principles of comfort, class and consistency, LUXTRIP was created to meet the demand for reliable luxury mobility in Kenya. From booking to drop-off, every detail is handled with care to ensure a first-class journey.
